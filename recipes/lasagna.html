Homemade Lasagna

Description

Homemade lasagna is a perfect dish that is a real comfort during cold winter months.

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Lasagna Noodles

Steps

  1. Brown beef
  2. Layer beef, sauce, cheese, noodles until it's all used up
  3. Bake at 350 for 45 minutes
