Homemade Lasagna

Description

There are many types of lasagna one could make, but a classic homemade recipe can't be beat!

Ingredients

  • Ground Beef
  • Onion
  • Canned Tomatoes
  • Fresh herbs
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the meat
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the cheese layer
  4. Assemble the lasagna
  5. Bake the lasagna
