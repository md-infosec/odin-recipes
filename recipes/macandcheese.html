Mac and Cheese

Description

This dump and cook mac and cheese recipe is sure to be a favorite in your home!

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • Butter
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Canned Soup
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Boil pasta
  2. Stir in butter, seasonings, and half of the cheese
  3. Whisk milk and eggs together, stir in pasta
  4. Sprinkle remaining cheese overtop
    5. Cook on low for 3 1/2 hours
