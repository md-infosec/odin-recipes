Homemade Lasagna

Description

Good, old fashioned mashed potatoes are melt in your mouth delicious!

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Garlic
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Skin the potatoes and cube them
  2. Boil potatoes in water
  3. Saute the garlic in a separate pan
  4. Add milk, butter, seasonings to the garlic pan
  5. Drain potatoes
  6. Mix all ingredients together
